Dame Agatha Christie

Queen of Mystery

She also wrote the world's longest-running play, The Mousetrap, which was performed in the West End from 1952 to 2020. She also wrote other non-crime novels under a psuedonymn "Mary Westmacott." Her works have been dramatised into several productions, most famous being the ones produced by BBC.
